Loan Calculation Methods

Repayment (EMI)

The monthly payment remains constant throughout the term.

$$M = P \times \frac{r(1+r)^n}{(1+r)^n-1}$$

Decreasing (Equal Principal)

The principal is divided equally, and interest decreases over time.

$$M_t = \frac{P}{n} + (P - \frac{P}{n} \times (t-1)) \times r$$

Interest Only

You only pay the interest charges each month; the principal remains the same.

$$M = P \times r$$
